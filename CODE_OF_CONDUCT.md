# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Vouch community a welcoming and positive experience for everyone — regardless of age, background, experience level, gender identity, nationality, race, religion, or any other personal characteristic.

We pledge to act in ways that contribute to an open, inclusive, and respectful community.

---

## Our Standards

**We encourage:**

- Being kind and patient with others
- Welcoming different perspectives and experiences
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully
- Acknowledging mistakes and learning from them
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**We do not tolerate:**

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Discriminatory jokes or language
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Dismissing or belittling contributions based on experience level
- Any other conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all community spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other venue where community members represent the project.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it by opening a private issue or contacting the maintainers directly. All reports will be handled with discretion and taken seriously.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. Responses to violations may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community, depending on severity and context.
