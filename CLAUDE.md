# CLAUDE.md

## About Vouch

Vouch is building Business Decision Intelligence.

Mission:

Help people and businesses make better decisions from the data they already have.

The open-source edition is called:

Vouch Starter Kit

Its purpose is to allow anyone to upload business data and immediately discover useful insights.

---

## Development Philosophy

Build one feature completely before moving to the next.

Avoid unnecessary complexity.

Always prefer simplicity.

Every screen should answer one question:

"What deserves my attention?"
